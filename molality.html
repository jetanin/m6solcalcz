Molality Equation

\[C = \frac{Mol}{M_2} = \frac{M_1}{M_2} × \frac{1000}{MW.}\]

   C (molality)  : Concentration

     Mol (Mol)   : Number of Mol of Solvent

        M1 (g)      : Mass of solute

        M2 (g)      : Mass of solvent

      MW. (g)     : Molecular Mass of Solute
