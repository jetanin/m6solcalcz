Mol Equation

\[mol = \frac{M}{MW} = \frac{N}{6.02 × 10^{23}} = \frac{V}{22.4}\]

    mol   : Number of moles of solute (mol)

      M    : Mass of solvent (g)

    MW. : Molecular mass of solute (g)

      N    : number of particles of substance

      V    : Gas quantity (dm3 or liters at STP)
